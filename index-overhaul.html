Earth friendly INVERTER technology

What is it?

An inverter compressor is opperated at a percentage of full power as needed to maintain a set temperature. It differs from fixed-speed compresors where the compressor cycles ON/OFF. By only using the needed power inverter compressors saves energy and keeps the set temperature change minimal

Types of air Conditioning systems

Split wall

Consists of a seperate indoor and outdoor unit. Indoor unit is mounted on the wall.

Cassette

Indoor unit fits flush with the ceiling.

Ducted

Fits in the ceiling, flexible ducting conveys air to multiple rooms.

Under-ceiling

Wall mounted or ceiling suspended. Suited for comercial applications

Portable

Complete unit on wheels. small sized ducting for warm air outlet

Floor-standing

An upright standing unit. Well suited for areas with little to no wall space.
